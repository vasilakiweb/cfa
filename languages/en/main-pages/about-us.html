ABOUT US

Our story

The CFA Holding Group Ltd was founded in October 2015 by the long experience of the professionals who operate since 2000 in a deal of international co -working that involves more than 17 countries worldwide with 7 offices in Europe and sees its greatest expression in sectors Finance, Commodities and Business Consulting. The CFA Holding Group Ltd. is a Spin Off of the Corporate Financial Advisor Ltd set up in 2009 having the aim of creating a professional team that it can give concrete and effective answers to all the companies who entrust themselves to structure for their projects or development of their companies. The professionals who make up the structure come from different areas and each with a very specific experience, the aim of the holding company is to bring together these professionals concretely in a permanent staff in order to consolidate the work experience up to now and to develop share and strengthen certain areas of business consulting, not least open up new areas of action in order to differentiate the offer to the customer and be able to assist to 360 degrees for all those who may be his needs. The structuring in Divisions allows it to be dynamic in the customer management and provide advice and highly specialized solutions in addition to the different sectors in which they operate, sometimes they offer unique and innovative solutions, generating new business models. The professionality and the competence shown has meant that since the birth of this team has been involved in expert advice to institutions with banks or chambers of commerce of different European countries, this activity has led to the Memorandum of Understanding subscriptions or cooperation agreements international. The holding company is constantly expanding, and so it is always searching for professionals who espouse the idea of business and become members of the team in order to support their customers with unique services or make use of different professional experiences they might need.
