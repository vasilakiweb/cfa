Risk Management

Risk management is the process that allows to measure and estimate the risks that a company runs, identifying the most effective strategies to control them and limit them. Generally employed strategies may include the transfer of risk, prevention, reduction of the negative effect. Some typical cases are the natural disasters, fires, criminal trials, the financial risks.

Risk management is required by law and is regulated by Legislative Decree no. 231/01 which provides various kinds of penalties: fines, disqualification, confiscation, publication of the judgment.

  • Advice
  • Training
  • Legal assistance covered by insurance
  • Regular reports
  • Auditing
